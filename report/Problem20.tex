As $n_x$ is a band-limited noise characterized by $R(\tau)=\sigma^2e^{-\beta|\tau|}$, we have $\dot{n_x}=A_{nx}n_x+B_{nx}w$ with

\begin{equation*}
  A_{nx} = \begin{pmatrix}
   -\beta & 0 \\
	0 & -\beta
\end{pmatrix}
\qquad B_{bn} = \begin{pmatrix}

	\end{pmatrix}
\end{equation*}
